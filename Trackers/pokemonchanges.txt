=====================================================================
                              NEW ABILITIES
=====================================================================
NIGHTCAP - Contact with the Pokemon may put the foe to sleep, same rate as Poison Point.
VAMPIRE - Slightly powers up moves that drain HP, 1.3x base power.
SHRINK - Minimises the body if HP becomes half or less, adds minimise effect and ups evasion 2 levels.
COGWORKS - Ups Atk if another Pokémon joins the Cogworks, the same as Plus or Minus, but for special and physical moves.
WATERMILL - Sharply ups Speed when receiving a Water-type move, ups Speed 2 levels.
EXTERMINATE- Normal-type moves become Dark-type moves, standard -ate ability.
EVILSTANCE - Takes a stance that power's up each Pokémon's Poison-type moves, functionally the same as Dark/Fairy Aura.
HEROSTANCE - Takes a stance that power's up each Pokémon's Fighting-type moves, functionally the same as Dark/Fairy Aura.
PEACEKEEPER,Peacekeeper - Breaks "Stance" abilities to weaken affected moves, functionally the same as Aura Break.
EVAPORATE - Evaporates Water-type moves and boosts evasion, immune to Water moves and ups evasion 1 stage.
HOLLOWCORE - A hollow body leaves it immune to status conditions, total immunity.
RESOLUTEWING - Boosts Speed stat when the Pokémon enters a battle, ups Speed 1 stage when entering battle.
FIERYSURGE - Turns the ground into Fiery Terrain, functionally the same as Surge abilities.
COMBUSTER - Burns up Def and Sp.Def to boost Sp.Atk on Fiery Terrain, Def and Sp. Def are halved, Sp. Atk is doubled on Fiery Terrain.
BURNINGBODY - It's always burning, and attacks without extinguishing, functionally the same as Comatose.
TRICKSTER - Sets up a Trick Room when entering battle, what it says.
CLUTCH - Clutches a special item. Unable to use held items, like Klutz, but thematic.
FOGCALL - Whips up a fog to conceal the field when entering battle, sets Deep Fog weather condition for 5 turns.
CACOPHANY - Draws in sound moves to sharply boost Sp. Atk, functionally the same as Lightning Rod or Storm Drain, but for sound-moves.
ETERNALBODY - Contact moves have no effect, what it says, unique to Eternamax Eternatus.
FREEZER - Special moves may release a freezing gas, special moves may Freeze the attacker, 30% chance.
SOULDRAIN - Boosts Sp.Atk after knocking out any Pokémon, ups Sp.Atk 1 level on knockout.
LUNARSHELL - Creates Reflect and Lightscreen with the moon's energy, sets up screens for 5 turns on entry to battle.
FIRSTWIND - Swift winds give priority on the first turn, highest priority on first turn on battle.
REVERBERATE - Boosts power and resistance of sound-based moves, identical to Punk Rock.
ZENPOWER -  Raises the Pokémon's Special Attack stat, special version of Pure Power.
SILENTSTRIKE - Moves first when using blade-moves, +1 priority when using blade-moves.
SCAVENGER - Scavenges HP each time a Pokemon faints, when any Pokemon is knocked out, regains 1/3 HP.
ALLSEEINGEYE - Uses the minds eye to see a foe's true self in Fog, perfect accuracy and ups crit rate 1 level in Fog.
MYSTERYZONE - Mysterious potential in Fog boosts the power of special moves, boosts base power of special moves x1.5 in Fog.
CAROLSONG - Its sound moves become Ice-type. Boosts power in unison, sound moves are Ice-type, the same as Plus or Minus.
CLOUDBURST - Draws on the energy of the storm to change form, Rosen Ampharos unique ability, changes to Electric/Flying in the Rain.
GARDENER - Powers up Grass-type moves, functionally the same as Steel Worker but for Grass moves.
MISTYCLOAK - Reduces all damage under the cloak of Fog, halves damage taken in Fog.
